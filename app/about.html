A bit more about me...

Teamwork

"If everyone is moving forward together, then success takes care of itself." During my University years, we had several subjects where we had to work in smaller groups. I have particularly enjoyed this experience, for the reason that with combined effort we were able to achieve greater results than individually.

Communication

I have extensive customer-facing work experience. As a former call centre agent and hotel receptionist, I learnt how to communicate with colleagues, clients and superiors effectively in written and verbal manner. Both roles required strong listening skills in order to solve problematic situations.

Critical Thinking

I am always evaluating and carefully analysing the possible outcomes before I make a decision. When I build a website, I am often faced with several different implementation methods. In these situations I have to quickly analyse and test each method before I choose the right one.

Problem Solving

“Fall in love with problems, then with solutions.” I believe, in web development & UXD, every product is a solution for someone else’s problem. Therefore it is vital to understand the users (and their problems) in order to create outstanding digital experience.

Agile Mindset

“There is no failure, only feedback” When I am facing, something particularly challenging, I always take it as an opportunity to learn something new. This mentality helps to keep my positive attitude and overcome obstacles.

Multilingual

“To have another language is to possess a second soul.” I was originally born and raised in Hungary, moved to UK in 2012. My first language is Hungarian, I have a good command of English and a basic level of German.
